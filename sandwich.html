Ingredients

  • Bread slices
  • Mayonnaise
  • Vegetables (carrots, capsicum,nion, sweet corn, lettuce, cucumber, avocado, cabbage,etc.)
  • Butter
  • 1 Pinch Salt

Instructions

  1. Prepare ingredients as mentioned in the list.
  2. In a mixing bowl, add mayonnaise, chopped veggies, and seasonings. Mix everything well.
  3. Apply some butter to both slices of bread.
  4. Spread veg-mayo mixture generously on one slice of bread slice.
  5. Then, cover and press with another bread slice over it. Cut the sandwich into triangles/squares.
Time Required:10 minutes
